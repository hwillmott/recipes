Ingredients:
2 3/4 c plus 1 tbsp flour (415 g)
1 1/4 tsp baking soda
1 1/4 tsp salt
1 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp ground ginger
1/4 tsp ground cloves
1 1/4 c unsalted butter, room temp (235 g)
1 1/4 c packed light brown sugar (245 g)
1/2 c plus 2 tbsp granulated sugar
2 large eggs plus 1 egg yolk
1 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
1/3 c unsulfured molasses (100 g)
1 3/4 c coursely chopped white chocolate (280 g)

Steps:
1. preheat oven to 350 F, line 12x17 baking tray with parchment
2. whisk flour, soda, salt, and spices in a bowl
3. in electric mixer with paddle attachment, beat butter and sugars on medium-high speed until creamy and pale, about 3 minutes
4. add eggs and yolk one at a time, scraping down sides of bowl as neeed
5. add vanilla and molasses and mix on medium speed until combined
6. add flour mixture on low speed until combined
7. stir in white chocolate
8. spread batter evenly into pan and bake until golden, about 25 minutes
