NOTE: must be chilled

Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. plus 1 tbsp flour
1 1/4 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp ground cloves
1/4 tsp freshly grated nutmeg
1 tbsp cocoa powder
1/4 lb unsalted butter, room temp
1 1/2 tsp freshly grated peeled ginger
1/2 c. packed dark brown sugar
1/2 c. unsulfured molasses
1 tsp baking soda
1 1/2 tsp boiling water
7 oz. semisweet chocolate, cut into chunks
1/4 c. granulated sugar

Steps:
1. line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper
2. sift together flour, ground ginger, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, and cocoa
3. in electric mixer with paddle attachment, beat butter and fresh ginger on medium until lighteneed, about 4 minutes
4. add brown sugar, beat until combined
5. add molasses, beat until combined
6. in a small bowl, dissolve baking soda in boiling water
7. beat half of flour mixture into butter mixture
8. beat in baking soda mixture, then rest of flour mixture
9. mix in chocolate
10. turn onto plastic wrap, pat out into 1-inch thickness, seal, refrigerate until firm (2 hrs or overnight)
11. preheat oven to 325
12. roll dough into 1 1/2 inch balls, place 2 inches apart on baking sheets
13. chill 20 minutes
14. roll in granulated sugar
15 bake until surfaces just begin to crack, 10-12 minutes, rotating halfway
