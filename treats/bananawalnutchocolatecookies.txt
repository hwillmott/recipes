Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. flour
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 c. granulated sugar
1/2 c. packed light brown sugar
1 egg
1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 c. mashed ripe banana (about 1 large)
1 c. old-fashined rolled oats
8 oz. semisweet chocolate, coarsely chopped
1/2 c. coarsely chopped walnuts (about 2 oz.)

Steps:
1. preheat oven to 375
2. whisk together flour, salt, baking soda in bowl
3. in electric mixture, mix together butter and sugars on medium until pale and fluffy
4. reduce speed to low and add egg and vanilla, mix until combined
5. mix in banana
6. add flour mixture, mix until just combined
7. stir in oats, chocolate, and walnuts
8. scoop out dough onto baking sheets in 1 1/2 inch balls
9. bake for about 12 minutes, rotating sheets halfway through
