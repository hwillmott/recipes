1 box pasta (fettuccini, spaghetti, bowties are all good)
1/2 c. milk
1 egg
salt and pepper
1-2 Tbsp Garlic Mrs. Dash
1/2 c. chopped parsley
1/2 c. grated cheddar
2/3 c. grated reggiano parmesan
1 pack cooked bacon (cut into lardons, fry until cooked but still soft, wrap in paper towels to absorb grease)

Steps:
1. cook the bacon
2. mix milk and egg together with fork, then add seasoning, parsley, and cheese
3. cook the pasta (can do this while cooking bacon)
4. drain pasta
5. put sauce in bottom of pan
6. put pasta back into pan, mix until cheese is all melted and spread throughout, mix in bacon
