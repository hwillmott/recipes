2 kg cherry tomatoes (different colours if possible)
2 sprigs fresh thyme
2 sprigs rosemary
2 fresh bay leasves
1 tbsp dried oregano
3 cloves garlic, chopped
12 mild italian or other non-breakfast sausage
olive oil
balsamic vinegar
salt & pepper

1. Preheat oven to 375 F
2. Put all veggies + fresh herbs into the casserole dish
3. Prick the sausages with a fork or knife, add to dish
4. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar
5. Season with salt + pepper, oregano
6. Mix it all around with your hands until everything is coated nicely, then arrange sausages on top of tomatoes
7. Bake for half an hour, take out, turn sausages
8. Bake for another 15-30 minutes, until brown to liking (can use broiler to crisp)
