In a bag overnight:
3-5 lb. chicken thighs
2-4 garlic cloves
3 tbsp oregano
1/2 c. olive oil
1/2 c. red wine vinegar
1/4 c. green olives
1/4 c. capers
1/4 c. dried prunes
1/4 c. dried apricots
1/4 c. dried figs
2 bay leaves

The day of:
put everything into a casserole dish (maybe remove some chicken skin so it's less greasy)
squeeze lemon onto everything
salt and pepper
bake at 350 F, basting frequently

Serve with couscous and green beans
