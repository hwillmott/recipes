2 lb. lean ground beef
2-3 large onions, finely chopped
1 jalapeno pepper
2 green peppers
2 garlic cloves, crushed
28 oz. diced tomatoes
1 c. beef stock
1 c. water
1 can tomato paste
2 cans black beans
2 cans kidney beans
1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
1 tsp worchestershire sauce
2 tbsp chili powder + ancho chili powder?
2 tbsp ground cumin
1/4 tsp allspice
1/4 - 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp weber steak spice
salt and pepper

1. brown the beef 
2. add the onions and garlic
3. add all remaining ingredients and bring to gentle simmer
4. loosely cover and simmer for 30-40 minutes
