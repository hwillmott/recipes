Bianca's Ragu

1 clove garlic
1 carrot
1 tbsp onion
grate these finely

3 tbsp olive oil
1 kg beef
2 leaves sage
1 sprig rosemary
4 leaves basil
1 c. white wine
pinch sugar
s + p
4 cans tomato puree

1. fry garlic, carrot, onion, olive oil
2. add beef
3. when beef is partly cooked, add wine
4. when most of liquid has edvaporated, add tomatoes. Then add spices and simmer for 1 hour
