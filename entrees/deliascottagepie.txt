1 lb. ground beef
2 medium onions
1 large carrot
1/2 tsp cinnamon (can use less)
1/2 tsp dried mixed herbs
2 tsp weber steak spice
1 tbsp fresh parsley
1 tbsp plain flour
1 tbsp tomato puree
10 oz beef stock
mashed potatoes for top

* double the recipe for a large casserole

1. preheat oven to 400 F
2. in a large frying pan, fry onions and brown a little
3. add carrots + beef and brown
4. add seasonings
5. stir in flour
6. mix puree and stock
7. add to meat and bring to a simmer
8. into the casserole dish: meat, mash, grated cheese
9. bake for 25 minutes
