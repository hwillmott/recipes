Pastitsio 

1 1/2 lb ground beef
1/4 tsp pepper
8 oz elbow macaroni
1/8 tsp cinnamon
3 tbsp butter
1/8 tsp oregano
1/4 c. flour
10 3/4 ox can tomato puree
3 c. milk
1 lg. onion, finely chopped
3 eggs
1 tsp salt
1/2 c. grated parmesan

1. preheat oven to 375
2. cook macaroni, rinse with cold water, drain
3. stir flour into melted butter in heavy saucepan. Gradually stir in milk and cook until thickened. Cover and cool.
4. Brown ground beef and onion in large frying pan. Pour off drippings. Sprinkle salt, pepper, cinnamon, oregano over beef.
5. Stir in tomato puree and cook slowly for 10 minutes.
6. Beat together egg, 1/3 c. parmesan. Gently stir in macaroni. Gradually stir in 2 c. white sauce.
7. Place 1/2 macaroni mixture into buttered baking dish.
8. Spoon beef mixture in even layer on top. 
9. Put rest of macaroni on top
10. Pour over remaining white sauce.
11. Bake in oven for 20 minutes. Sprinkle with rest of parmesan, bake for 20-25 minutes more, or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.
12. Let stand for 12 minutes.
