1 whole cooked chicken
1 can cream of celery soup
same quantity of milk
grated cheddar
Mrs. Dash yellow lid
2-3 heads of broccoli plus stems
mashed potatoes for topping

1. preheat oven to 375 
2. cook the broccoli
3. mix the milk and the soup in the casserole dish
4. add a generous amount of Mrs. Dash
5. add chicken and broccoli and stir
6. sprinkle cheese on top
7. cover with mashed potatoes
8. bake until golden on top
