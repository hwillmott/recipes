Ingredients:
giblets + neck of turkey
1 onion, halved
1 1/2 pts cold water
a few parsley stalks
a chunk of celery and a few leaves
1 bay leaf
6 whole black peppercorns
salt

Steps:
1. Wash giblets
2. put in saucepan and cover with water and bring to simmer
3. remove surface scum
4. add remaining ingredients
5. half-cover and simmer for 1 1/2 - 2 hrs
6. strain and bring up to boiling point again before adding to gravy
