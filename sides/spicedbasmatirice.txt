425 ml basmati rice
3 tbsp vegetable oil
50 g onion, finely chopped
1/2 fresh hot green chili, finely chopped
1/2 tsp minced garlic
1/2 tsp garam masala
1 tsp salt
1 pint chicken stock

1. Wash rice several times. Soak for 30 min in 2 pts water
2. Drain in sieve for 20 min
3. Heat oil in heavy bottom saucepan on medium
4. Add onion and brown lightly
5. Add rice, green chili, garlic, garam masala and salt
6. Stir gently for 3-4 min until all grains are coated with oil. If rice begins to stick to bottom, turn down slightly.
7. Pour in stock and bring to boil. Cover with tight-fitting lid, turn down to very very low and cook for 25 min
