1 tbsp vegetable oil
1 yello wonion
2 leeks (grren part only)
1 garlic bulb
4 carrots
4 celery sticks
1-2 apples
4 bay leaves
1 tbsp peppercorns
1 tbsp coriander seeds
1 tbsp coarse salt
few sprigs: thyme, parsley, sage
20 cups cold water

1. peel and roughly chop onions, leeks, carrots, celery
2. smash garlic cloves
3. quarter the apples
4. heat oil and add onions, leeks, carrots, celery, salt, bay leaves
5. saute for 5 minutes, stirring often
6. add everything else, including the water, and bring to a boil
7. reduce heat and simmer gently for 45 minutes
8. strain and cool
